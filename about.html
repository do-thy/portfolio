About Me

I find joy in bringing things to life, whether through cooking delicious desserts or nurturing a garden. I am also simply fascinated by computers and technology in general. To think that we have easy access to these magical boxes that can do things beyond the comprehension of an average person from the 18th century, is just incredible. These passions are what drove me to pursue the art of programming.

My Aspirations

In the long run, I believe that being a well established software developer is my calling, as it aligns with the joy I find in creation. Currently, though, every fiber in my being is focused on preparing for my On-the-Job Training with the end goal of being absorbed as a full-fledged employee.
